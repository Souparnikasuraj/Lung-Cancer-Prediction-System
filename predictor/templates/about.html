About LungCare AI

LungCare AI is a machine learning-powered system designed to assist in early detection of lung cancer by analyzing user health and lifestyle data. Our goal is to make prediction fast, accessible, and reliable.

We aim to build a future where early detection of lung cancer becomes simple and accessible. By combining Artificial Intelligence with healthcare, we strive to support better decision-making and improve lives.

What We Focus On

AI Prediction

Using machine learning models to predict lung cancer risk efficiently.

Accessibility

Providing an easy-to-use platform for users everywhere.

Health Impact

Helping users take early action and improve health outcomes.

Research Driven

Built using data-driven insights and ML experimentation.

How It Works

Users input details such as age, smoking habits, and lifestyle factors. The system processes this data using trained models like Logistic Regression and Random Forest to predict lung cancer risk.

Technologies Used

Frontend: HTML, CSS, Bootstrap
Backend: Django (Python)
Machine Learning: Scikit-learn
Database: SQLite
